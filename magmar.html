MagMaR
 

To the "educated" middle-schooler, the abbreviation for the Mission Math Regional (MagMaR) seems to share its name with a certain Pokemon. We hope to make this contest as distinguished and exciting for its participants as possible, while providing an instructive opportunity for everyone. While the details of the involvement of Pokemon with the contest theme will not be revealed until the contest itself, we assure that no math problem will require any knowledge of Pokemon.

We hope this contest will be a unique experience for you!
