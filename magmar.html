MagMaR (Mission Math Regional)

About
 

The Mission San Jose High School Math Club is proud to host a middle school math competition for motivated math competitors in the Bay Area from 6th to 8th grade. The contest introduces a unique contest format and an appealing theme to traditional math competitions.

To the "educated" middle-schooler, the abbreviation for the Mission Math Regional (MagMaR) seems to share its name with a certain Pokemon. We hope to make this contest as exciting for its participants as possible, while providing an instructive opportunity for everyone. While the details of the involvement of Pokemon with the contest theme will not be revealed until the contest itself, we assure that no math problem will require any knowledge of Pokemon.

We hope this contest will be a unique experience for you!
