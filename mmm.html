MMT is a fun math tournament written by MSJ Math Club, MSJ alumni, and guest writers, and run by MSJ Math Club. It also acts as the Team Selection Test for Mission's SMT and BMT teams.

Registration
 
(registration will be open soon!) Participation in the full contest is $5 per person. Teams may be up to 4 people in size, and the maximum total AIME score is 20 (per team). People who have not yet qualified for AIME count as 0 towards the total AIME score. If you wish only to participate for SMT/BMT team selection, then you make choose to take only the regular round, which is free.
