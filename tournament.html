This is the MMM homepage!

MMM (Mission Math Meet) is the annual math intramural written by students at MSJ!

MMM aims to maximize interest in math and math problems, increase the types of problems students are exposed to, and encourage cooperation in problem solving.

MMM also acts as the team selection test for the MSJ SMT and BMT teams.

The MMM is written by MSJ Math Club, MSJ alumni, and guest writers, and run by MSJ Math Club.
