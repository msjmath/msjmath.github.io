First Meeting! ~Austin
 
Welcome to a new year of impossibly complex algebra, really bashy geometry problems, casework, and delicious pizza! It will be a fun year with a mixture of competition math, recreational mathematics, and even a bit of "higher-level" math, hopefully with something new for everyone. To avoid conflicts with other clubs (we want you to try out different clubs too, especially freshmen!), our first math club meeting will be on Wednesday (September 3) in C5 at lunch. However, all subsequent weekly math club meetings will be on Thursdays in C5 at lunch, unless otherwise specified.

If you are interested in joining MSJ Math Club and you have not signed up, please fill out this form here.

P.S. In addition, we'd really appreciate it if you could take some time to fill out this survey. As officers, we'd like to know what members of Math Club are interested in, so we can better plan out our events/lectures and what level they should be at. Also, if you know anyone who'd be interested in Math Club, let them know that they can still sign up here.
