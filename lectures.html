Mass Points~Alvin
 
Mass points is a technique that simplifies the process of finding ratios between lengths of segments in a geometric problem, and it is actually based on the concept of torque (from physics). Problems that use mass points can usually be solved using similar triangles, area ratios, or vector bashing, but mass points gives us a cleaner, faster solution.

Vieta's Formulas~Austin
 
Vieta's Formulas (also called Viete's Formulae) are a quick way to determine the sum, product, etc. of the roots of a polynomial. They are closely related to the symmetric polynomials, which form and entry point into Galois theory and some topics in modern algebra.
